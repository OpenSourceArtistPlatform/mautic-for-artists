# The Open Source Artist Platform

## Beyond Email Marketing

Mautic for Artists gives you a powerful email marketing foundation. But there's a lot more to building an independent music career online.

**The Open Source Artist Platform** is a fully managed, multi-service platform built specifically for musicians. It brings together everything you need under one roof:

| Service | What It Does | Open Source Project |
|---------|--------------|---------------------|
| Email Marketing | Fan list, campaigns, automation | [Mautic](https://mautic.org/) |
| Website & Blog | Your artist website | [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) |
| Social Media | Schedule posts across platforms | [Mixpost](https://mixpost.app/) |
| Short Links | Track link clicks | [YOURLS](https://yourls.org/) |
| Analytics | Privacy-friendly visitor analytics | [Matomo](https://matomo.org/) |
| Community | Fan forums and discussion | [Flarum](https://flarum.org/) |

## Why Managed?

Self-hosting is great for control and learning. But managing multiple services, keeping them updated, handling security patches, SSL certificates, backups, and uptime monitoring takes real time and expertise.

The managed platform handles:

- **Automatic updates** — Security patches and version upgrades applied automatically
- **Daily backups** — Your data is backed up daily with 90-day retention
- **SSL certificates** — HTTPS configured and renewed automatically
- **Monitoring** — 24/7 uptime monitoring with alerting
- **Support** — Direct support from the team that built it
- **Integration** — All services connected: email subscribers sync with your website, social links are tracked, analytics are unified

## Who It's For

The managed platform is for artists who want to:

- Focus on making music, not managing servers
- Have a professional online presence without hiring a web team
- Own their fan data (not rent it from social platforms)
- Use open source tools without the DevOps overhead
